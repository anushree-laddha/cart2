Our Story

Founded in 2023, CART began with a simple idea: to make grocery shopping as effortless as possible. We noticed how time-consuming and frustrating traditional grocery shopping could be, and we set out to change that.

Our platform combines the convenience of online shopping with smart features that help you discover new products, remember your favorites, and checkout in seconds.

Today, we serve thousands of happy customers across Pune, with plans to expand nationwide. Our commitment to quality, convenience, and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.
